How to play the game

Use the chat box to talk to your opponent. Ask questions to try to guess who their person is.
When you think you have it narrowed down, enter the name of your guess in the GUESS WHO box below the chat box.
If you're right, you win. If you're wrong feel free to keep playing and guess again.
